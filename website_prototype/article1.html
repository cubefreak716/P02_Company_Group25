Welcome to Valhalla

This is a new game we are making. As you can guess, it's about vikings and stuff. As you would expect, this game will involve running around, climbing things and murdering people. We expect it to be very fun. You will be able to customize your character's equipment, which is an exciting system.

Equipment customization comes in many forms. While you have a variety of items to choose from in terms of what to wear and wield, you can also create new items! The in depth crafting system lets you mix and max components to create your ideal weapon or armour. This will allow you to create items that perfectly suit your playstyle, whether you prefer being fast and hard to hit, or slow but hard to hurt.

In addition, the painting system will let you colour the items to match your style, and some emblems will be able to placed on suitable equipment such as shields and armour. Make your mark in the fashion world with the in depth customization available to you!
