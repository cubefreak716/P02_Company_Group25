About Us

What is Phalanx Studios

Phalanx Studios is a game development studio. While we make a variety of games, we have a focus on historical fiction and roleplaying games. We strive for quality, and maintain close ties with the community to pursue this goal. We take feedback to heart, and use it to improve our games.

Company Ethos

We strive to connect players through the amazing world of gaming. We want to provide gamers with the best experience possible and are constantly updating our games to create fresh environments.

Notable achievments

Phalanx Studios has won best gaming company award since 2017. In 2018, Phalanx's "Robo Chicken" was voted best game of the year.
