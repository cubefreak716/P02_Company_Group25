Overview: This company's purpose is to display the availability of essential products that are hard to find due to the ongoing pandemic by location.This webpage's primary audience are the elderly and those who have trouble accessing these products on their own.

Branding Ideas

Company's Mission

To provide a clean and intuitive webpage that easily allows users to find local stores that sell essential producs during the COVID19 pandemic.

Who this webpage is for

The primary stakeholders are the elderly and other citizens who have a difficult time getting essential items on their own.

Why is this company necessary

Due to an increasing amount of misinformation being spread and a continuing concern for product shortages, many people are finding it difficult to find some essential products in their neighborhood. The company would theorectically provide a hub where users can easily locate which store has the product they are looking for in stock.

User Tasks

User will be able to:

  1. View what the essential products the company is tracking
  2. View which stores selling essential products and check their availability
  3. Request a product to be put on hold
  4. Compare prices of products between stores
  5. Contact stores for further inquiry and request a new item to be tracked
